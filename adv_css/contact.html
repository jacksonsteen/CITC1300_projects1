Opinions on Dune: Part One

I think this movie executed everything it needed to do very well. It sets up the plot of the universe, introduces important concepts to the story, and gives an amazing place to continue the story. I believe this movie only becomes excellent when put beside it's sequel. For people who enjoy loads of action and are less interested in the story and development of concepts this movie might feel boring or even lackluster. However, if you have the patience to learn and understand everything as well as be open-minded to the payoff that is to come, then this movie leaves you excited for the next film; and this excitement will definitely be a payoff for you. Overall, I would give this movie anywhere in the range of a 8-9/10 as it is very visually stunning, immersive, and well-written.

Brief Overview of Dune: Part One

Dune: Part One begins by introducing the main cast as well as setting up the pivotal change to House Atreides which is being tasked with the oversight of the planet called Arrakis. We learn that Arrakis is the most important planet in the entire universe as it provides spice, a resource used by all people for interstellar travel. Once the Atreides arrive on Arrakis we immediately see how hostile the relationship between them and the Harkonnens, the people who controlled Arrakis prior to their arrival, are. The Harkonnens have essentially sabatoged all spice-mining operations and even placed assassins to attempt to kill members of House Atreides. After seeing hints of Paul Atreides, the son of the house's leader, being a prophet in the eyes of the locals, they are caught in a surprise attack led by the Harkonnens with the assistance of the Emporer's personal army; this shows the task placed on House Atreides was a trap from the beginning. The leader and army of House Atreides are killed and Paul along with his mother and Duncan Idaho barely escape with their lives into the desert. The film ends with Paul winning a duel that allows him and his mother to be accepted into the ranks of the Fremen.
